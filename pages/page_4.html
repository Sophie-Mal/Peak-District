Accommodation

Ashbourne

Ashbourne, known as the "Gateway to the Peak District," is a charming market town perfect for camping enthusiasts.
It offers easy access to stunning landscapes and outdoor activities like hiking and cycling, with nearby attractions such as Dovedale and the Tissington Trail.
Campers can enjoy a range of campsites, from well-equipped to rustic, all within reach of Ashbourne's amenities, including shops, pubs, and restaurants.
The town's historic charm, combined with its vibrant community and events, makes Ashbourne an ideal base for exploring the natural beauty of the Peak District.

Bretton Cottage B&B

Bretton Cottage, located in the Peak District, is a charming accommodation offering stunning views and a serene escape.
Nestled in picturesque countryside, it provides a perfect base for exploring nearby attractions such as Eyam and the Hope Valley.
The cottage blends rustic charm with modern comforts, featuring cozy interiors and well-equipped facilities.
Guests can enjoy nearby hiking and cycling trails, as well as the tranquil beauty of the surrounding landscapes.
With its idyllic setting and easy access to outdoor activities, Bretton Cottage is an ideal retreat for a peaceful getaway in the Peak District.

Broadoak Hotel

The Broadoak Hotel, situated in the heart of the Peak District, offers comfortable accommodation with easy access to the region's attractions.
Featuring cozy rooms and modern amenities, it provides a relaxing retreat for visitors exploring the nearby towns and countryside.
The hotel's central location makes it convenient for guests to visit popular destinations like Bakewell and Chatsworth House.
With its warm hospitality and convenient facilities, the Broadoak Hotel is an ideal choice for travelers seeking a comfortable stay while exploring the beauty of the Peak District.
