Visit The Peak District

Facilities

Tourist Information Centre

The Tourist Information Centre in the Peak District serves as a valuable resource for visitors, providing information and assistance to enhance their experience in the region.
Located in a convenient spot, it offers guidance on attractions, activities, and accommodations across the Peak District.
Staffed by knowledgeable locals, the center can help visitors plan their itineraries, recommend hiking trails, and suggest places to dine or stay.
Additionally, it often showcases local events, exhibitions, and festivals, enriching visitors' understanding of the area's culture and heritage.
Whether seeking adventure or relaxation, the Tourist Information Centre is a helpful hub for discovering all that the Peak District has to offer.

Carriages Bar & Restaurant

Carriages Bar and Restaurant, nestled in the heart of the Peak District, offers a delightful dining experience amid scenic surroundings.
With its cozy ambiance and charming decor, it provides a welcoming atmosphere for guests to unwind and enjoy a meal.
The restaurant serves a diverse menu featuring locally sourced ingredients and classic dishes, ensuring a memorable culinary experience.
Whether indulging in a hearty meal after a day of exploring or savoring a leisurely drink by the fireplace, Carriages provides a relaxing retreat for visitors to the Peak District.
