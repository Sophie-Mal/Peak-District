Natural Features

Carsington Water

Carsington Water, located in the Peak District, is a picturesque reservoir known for its serene beauty and diverse recreational activities.
Surrounded by rolling countryside, it offers scenic walking and cycling paths, including a popular 8-mile trail around the reservoir.
Visitors can enjoy a variety of water sports, such as sailing, windsurfing, and kayaking, making it a hub for outdoor enthusiasts.
The visitor center provides educational exhibits and a caf√© with stunning views of the water.
With its blend of natural beauty, outdoor activities, and family-friendly facilities, Carsington Water is a delightful destination for a relaxing and adventurous day out.

Stanage Edge

Stanage Edge, located in the Peak District, is an iconic gritstone escarpment renowned for its striking beauty and exceptional rock climbing opportunities.
Stretching for approximately four miles, it offers panoramic views of the surrounding moorlands and valleys.
Stanage Edge is a haven for climbers, featuring a wide range of routes suitable for all skill levels, from beginners to experts.
Besides climbing, it is a popular spot for hiking and bouldering, with scenic trails leading to its dramatic edges.
The breathtaking scenery, rich history, and challenging climbs make Stanage Edge a beloved destination for outdoor enthusiasts and nature lovers alike.
